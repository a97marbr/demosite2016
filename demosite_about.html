About FluffBear™

FluffBear™ is a children clothing line headquartered in Stockholm that currently focuses exclusively on children's clothing sector. The product line targets young and active kids that like outdoor activities and are not afraid to make a statment with their cloths. Reaching the young in Sweden with a firm grip of 20% of the market this premium brands is here to stay!
